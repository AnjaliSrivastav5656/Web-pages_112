Dr.A.P.J.Abdul.Kalam

<>1931-2015<>

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam BR was an Indian aerospace scientist and satatesman who served as the 11th president of Indian from 2002 to 2007. He was born and raised in Rameshwaram,Tamilnadu and studied physics and aerospace engineering.
